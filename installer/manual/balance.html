Check Balance

You can check your balance and recharge your account.

Checking the Balance

This topic describes how to check the balance.

Procedure

  1. Click Settings.
  2. Click Check Balance in left navigation tree.
  3. Click Check to check the balance.

Topping Up on Local

Procedure

  1. Click Settings.
  2. Click Check Balance in the left navigation tree.
  3. Enter the top-up card number in the Enter Service Shortcode text box.
  4. Click Send to top up.
