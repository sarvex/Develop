Why the Internet Cannot Be Accessed?

Context

NOTE:

  • Contact your network service provider if the wireless network-accessing service is not activated.
  • Acquire the network settings from the local network service provider.

Procedure

  1. Ensure that you are in the service area covered by the network signals.
  2. Ensure that you have activated the wireless network-accessing service.
  3. Ensure that the network settings meet the requirements of the local network service provider.

Why Phone Numbers Cannot Be Pasted?

Context

A valid phone number can contain only 0-9, #, *, and +. Confirm that no other character is in the cut or copied text.
