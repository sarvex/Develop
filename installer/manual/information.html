Operator and Product Information

You can view the operator and product information on the PocketInternet SUPER PRO 3G.

Viewing the Operator and Product Information

Context

NOTE:

The operator and product information of different types of terminal devices may differ.

Procedure

  • Click Settings.
  • Click Operator/Product Info in left navigation tree.
  • View the operator and product information of the terminal device.

Saving the Operator and Product Information

Context

NOTE:

You can save the operator and product information in a *.txt file.

Procedure

  • Click Settings.
  • Click Operator/Product Info in the left navigation tree.
  • View the operator and product information of the terminal device.
  • Choose Save.
