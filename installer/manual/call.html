Voice Call

Launch the PocketInternet SUPER PRO 3G before making or answering a call.

Making a Call

With the PocketInternet SUPER PRO 3G , you can make a call to a telephone or a mobile phone.

Context

You can use the PocketInternet SUPER PRO 3G to make a call by different methods:

  • Entering the phone number directly
  • Selecting the phone number from the phonebook
  • Through the text message
  • Through the call log

NOTE:

Take dialing by entering the phone number directly as an example.

Procedure

  1. Click Voice Call.
  2. Enter the phone number.
    • Click the number keys on the Voice Calls interface.
    • Press the number keys on the keyboard.
  3. Click Call icon or press Enter on the keyboard to dial.

Answering a Call

You can answer an incoming call with the PocketInternet SUPER PRO 3G.

Context

NOTE:

  • When there is an incoming call, the icon of the PocketInternet SUPER PRO 3G flashes in the system tray.
  • If the visual prompt is enabled, when there is an incoming call, the Prompt dialog box is displayed on the lower right corner of the screen. Then you can click OK to switch to Voice call interface

Procedure

  1. Click Voice Call.
  2. Click Call icon to answer the call. Click End icon to end the call.
